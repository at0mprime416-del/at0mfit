Before beginning any exercise program, consult with your physician or a qualified healthcare professional, especially if you have any medical conditions, injuries, or health concerns.

Assumption of Risk

Physical exercise involves inherent risks including, but not limited to:

  • Muscle strain, sprains, and soft tissue injury
  • Bone fractures and joint injuries
  • Cardiovascular events
  • Heat-related illness
  • In extreme cases, serious injury or death

By using AT0M FIT and participating in any training program provided through this platform, you acknowledge and voluntarily assume all risks associated with physical exercise and training activities.

Stop Training and Seek Medical Attention If

You must stop all exercise immediately and contact a medical professional if you experience:

  • Chest pain, pressure, or tightness
  • Severe shortness of breath
  • Dizziness, lightheadedness, or fainting
  • Heart palpitations or irregular heartbeat
  • Sudden severe pain in any area of the body
  • Unusual or extreme fatigue
  • Any symptom that does not feel right

Coach and AI Recommendations

Workout programs, nutrition guidance, and recovery recommendations provided by your coach or the AI coaching system are based on the information you provide. It is your responsibility to report any injuries, health conditions, or limitations accurately. Inaccurate information may result in programs that are inappropriate for your health status.

You retain full responsibility for executing any exercise safely and within your personal physical limits. If an exercise or activity causes pain or discomfort, stop and consult a medical professional before continuing.
